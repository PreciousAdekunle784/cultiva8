1. Who We Are

Our registered address is: Innovation Hub, University of Ibadan, Oyo State, Nigeria.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Provide Platform Services: Estimating carbon sequestration, predicting spoilage, matching you with buyers, processing payments.
  • Verify Farm Activity: Cross-referencing your reported practices with satellite imagery to ensure carbon credit integrity.
  • Generate Weather Alerts: Sending you notifications when weather conditions threaten your stored produce.
  • Improve Our Models: Using aggregated, anonymized farm and weather data to improve the accuracy of our carbon estimation and spoilage prediction algorithms.
  • Communication: Sending service notifications, payment confirmations, and important platform updates via SMS, email, or in-app notifications.
  • Security & Fraud Prevention: Detecting and preventing fraudulent farm registrations, duplicate accounts, or inflated carbon credit claims.
  • Compliance: Meeting legal and regulatory obligations under Nigerian law, including the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA) 2023 and the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR).

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Under the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023 and the NDPR, we process your personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Consent: You provide explicit consent when you create an account and submit farm data. You may withdraw consent at any time (see Section 9).
  • Contractual Necessity: Processing your data is necessary to deliver the services you have signed up for, including carbon estimation, spoilage prediction, and marketplace transactions.
  • Legitimate Interest: We have a legitimate interest in improving our algorithms, preventing fraud, and ensuring platform security, provided this does not override your fundamental rights.
  • Legal Obligation: We may process data as required by Nigerian law, including tax reporting and anti-money laundering regulations.

5. Data Sharing & Third Parties

5.1 Who We Share Data With

We share your data only when necessary to provide our services:

  • Carbon Credit Buyers: When you list credits on the Marketplace, buyers see your farm's region (state-level), carbon estimate, and verification status. They do not see your name, exact GPS coordinates, or personal contact details until you approve a transaction.
  • Produce Buyers: When the buyer matching system connects you with a nearby buyer, we share your crop type, quantity, and approximate distance. Your exact location and phone number are shared only after you confirm the match.
  • Payment Providers: Paystack, Flutterwave, and OPay receive your payment details to process disbursements. They operate under their own privacy policies.
  • Satellite & Weather API Providers: We send your farm's GPS coordinates to the European Space Agency (Sentinel-2), ISRIC SoilGrids, Open-Meteo, and NASA POWER to retrieve relevant data. These services receive coordinates only — no personal identifying information.

5.2 Who We Never Share Data With

  • Advertising networks or data brokers.
  • Social media platforms.
  • Any third party for direct marketing purposes without your explicit consent.
  • Government agencies, except when compelled by a valid court order or legal process.

5.3 Aggregated & Anonymized Data

We may share aggregated, anonymized data with research institutions, development organizations (e.g., FAO, World Bank), and climate initiatives. This data cannot be used to identify individual farmers and is used solely for advancing agricultural research and climate policy.

6. Satellite & Geolocation Data

Farm GPS coordinates are central to how Cultiv8 works. Here's how we handle this sensitive data:

  • Collection: You provide coordinates by clicking on a map or entering them manually. We also accept WhatsApp location sharing.
  • Usage: Coordinates are used to pull satellite imagery (NDVI analysis), soil data, and hyperlocal weather forecasts.
  • Storage: Coordinates are stored in encrypted form on our servers. They are never displayed publicly.
  • Buyer Visibility: Buyers on the Marketplace see only your state and local government area — never your exact farm coordinates.
  • Deletion: If you delete your account, all GPS data associated with your farms is permanently deleted within 30 days.

7. Data Storage & Security

We take the security of your data seriously. Our measures include:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256).
  • Access Control: Employee access to personal data is restricted on a need-to-know basis and protected by multi-factor authentication.
  • Infrastructure: Our servers are hosted on cloud infrastructure with ISO 27001 certification, with data centers in Africa where available.
  • Regular Audits: We conduct regular security audits and penetration testing.
  • Incident Response: In the event of a data breach, we will notify affected users and the Nigeria Data Protection Commission (NDPC) within 72 hours, as required by the NDPA 2023.

9. Your Rights

Under the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023, you have the following rights:

  • Right of Access: You may request a copy of all personal data we hold about you. We will respond within 30 days.
  • Right to Rectification: You may request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data at any time through your account settings or by contacting us.
  • Right to Erasure: You may request deletion of your account and all associated data. We will complete deletion within 30 days, except for data we are legally required to retain.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You may request that we limit how we process your data while a dispute or verification is pending.
  • Right to Data Portability: You may request your Farm Data and transaction history in a machine-readable format (CSV or JSON).
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time. This does not affect the lawfulness of processing conducted before withdrawal.
  • Right to Object: You may object to processing based on legitimate interest. We will cease processing unless we demonstrate compelling legitimate grounds.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@Cultiv8.ng or call +234 (0) 812 000 0000. We may ask you to verify your identity before processing a request.

If you believe your data protection rights have been violated, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Nigeria Data Protection Commission (NDPC) at ndpc.gov.ng.

10. Children's Privacy

Cultiv8 is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we become aware that a child under 18 has provided us with personal data, we will delete that information immediately. If you believe a minor has registered on our platform, please contact us at privacy@Cultiv8.ng.

11. Cookies & Tracking

The Cultiv8 web application uses minimal cookies and tracking:

  • Essential Cookies: Session management and authentication. These are strictly necessary and cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics: We use privacy-focused analytics (no Google Analytics) to understand how the platform is used. All analytics data is anonymized and stored on our own infrastructure.
  • No Third-Party Trackers: We do not use advertising cookies, social media pixels, or any third-party tracking scripts.

The Cultiv8 USSD service does not use cookies of any kind.

12. International Data Transfers

Your data is primarily stored on servers within Africa. In cases where data must be processed outside Nigeria (e.g., satellite imagery analysis via European Space Agency infrastructure), we ensure adequate safeguards are in place, including:

  • Standard contractual clauses approved by the NDPC.
  • Processing only non-personally-identifiable data (GPS coordinates without associated names or phone numbers).
  • Encryption of all data in transit.
